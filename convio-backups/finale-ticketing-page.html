Join Sam, his crew, and MJFF leadership for an evening to celebrate the conclusion of Tour de Fox and Sam’s 3 month cross country journey. 

The celebration will be held at the observation deck of the Vancouver Lookout.

The night will feature comments from Sam Fox and Foundation leadership;
along with musical talent from Bend Sinister.

Tickets are $50 per guest with all proceeds going directly to The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson’s Research.
