Dear Friend,

The Tour de Fox has two goals: to rally the national Parkinson’s community into action and to raise $1 million for critical research. With your support, we can make a difference for the more than 5 million individuals living with Parkinson’s disease today.

This summer, in honor of my mom and all those touched by this disease, I’ll be biking thousands of miles and climbing 48 state high-points back to back in just over three months. But without you, none of it will matter. Through your support, participation and giving Tour de Fox will push the needle toward a cure.

For a limited time, every donation to Tour de Fox will be matched dollar-for-dollar thanks to the generosity of an MJFF board member. With 100% of Tour de Fox donations going straight to Parkinson’s research efforts worldwide, there is no better time to play a part in progress.

From all of us here at MJFF and from my family to yours, thank you for your support, however you choose to find your summit with Tour de Fox.

Sincerely,

Sam Fox
