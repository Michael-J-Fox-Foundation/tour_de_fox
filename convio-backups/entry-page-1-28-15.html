about tour de fox

Extreme adventure athlete and MJFF staff member Sam Fox (no relation to Michael) will bike and hike his way across the United States — climbing the highest peak in each state — in just three months... and he wants you to join him.

Tour de Fox unites and inspires the Parkinson's community through passion, sweat and determination all while raising critical funds for better Parkinson's treatments.

Be a part of the 14,000 mile journey for Parkinson's research. Find your summit with Tour de Fox by biking, climbing or celebrating with Sam.
