Climb the Grouse Grind - 3,614 ft.

September 12, 2015

The Grouse Grind is a world-famous, steep, winding trail up Grouse Mountain just north of Vancouver, BC. Trail is so steep in sections that vertical ladders dot the route. While a challenging climb, don't worry about the descent - there is a cable car to take you back to the parking lot. Enjoy a refreshment and sweeping views of downtown Vancouver from the top.

When: September 12, 2015. 11 AM

Where: Grouse Grind Trailhead. Grouse Mountain Resort. 6400 Nancy Greene Way, North Vancouver, BC. View map

The route: 2-mile one way hike on very steep well maintained trail that includes stair cases and log ladders. There is also a tram to the top for those unable to make the climb.

climb with sam

No hiking or climbing experience is necessary. Climbers should be in good physical condition and should be prepared for unpredictable weather and muddy trail conditions.
