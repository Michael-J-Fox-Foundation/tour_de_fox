Please join the Tour de Fox Wine Country Edition Post-Ride Festival at the Kendall Jackson Wine Center immediately following the Wine Country Ride.

Tickets are $30 per guest with all proceeds going directly to The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson’s Research.

Each ticket includes a commemorative glass, three drink tickets for wine or beer and a chicken paella lunch. Enjoy live music by American Honey starting at noon, great raffle and auction items as well as free parking.

Tickets will be available for purchase day of, but please purchase in advance.
