about tour de fox

donate to sam

Extreme adventure athlete and MJFF staff member Sam Fox will bike and hike his way across the USA — climbing the highest peak in each state — in just three months. His goal is to raise $1 million for critical Parkinson’s research – with another $500K in matching funds.

tour schedule

We're coming to your town — With stops in each of the lower 48 states the Tour de Fox aims to unite and inspire people from every corner of the Parkinson's community through passion, sweat, and determination.

ways to get involved

Join Sam for a part of the Tour! This 14,000 mile journey for Parkinson's research offers countless ways to be involved. Find your summit with Tour de Fox by biking, climbing or celebrating with Sam.
