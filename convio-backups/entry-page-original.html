From May to August 2014, adventurist and Michael J. Fox Foundation staff member Sam Fox (no relation to Michael J. Fox) is undertaking Tour de Fox, an extreme endurance expedition in support of Parkinson’s research. In just 3 months, Sam, 26, will traverse 14,000 miles from Maine to Vancouver — passing through all 48 of the lower contiguous United States, biking over 5,000 miles of the route and climbing the highest peak in each state. The goal: Unite and inspire Team Fox members, Foundation supporters and the PD community, and raise $500,000 for Parkinson’s research. Listen to a message from Michael J. Fox about the Tour.
